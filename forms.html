RPL Form For Domestic Workers

Please enter your full name as it appears on your identification documents.
Select your date of birth from the calendar.
Enter a valid email address. This will be used for further communication.
Provide a phone number where you can be reached.
Select your county of residence.
Select your subcounty of residence.
Describe any formal or informal education and training you have received.
List your previous jobs, the types of work you did, and how long you worked there. If there's no work experience just write "None".
Mention any special skills or abilities you have, such as cooking, cleaning, or child care.
Indicate the languages you can speak For example Kiswahili, English, Mother tongue e.t.c.
Provide the names and contact information for people who can testify to your work experience.
Attach any relevant documentation or materials to support your application Such as a letter of recommendation or certificates. You can attach multiple files.
